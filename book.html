About This Book

This book is a collection of the darkest parts of my mind throughout my 20s. The ebbs and flows of my mental health journey and the journey to coming back to myself again.

The themes include addiction, withdrawal, mental health, and recovery. By sharing this, I can release the feelings this journey brought up to move on into my future.

I hope this collection of poems shows others that they aren't alone. There's still a stigma regarding mental health. It's hard for people to recognize what they cannot see, and mental health cannot always be seen.

I see you and I believe in you.

⚠️ Content Note

This book contains themes of mental health struggles, addiction, depression, and recovery. Some content may be triggering for sensitive readers. Please prioritize your wellbeing while reading.

Coming Back to Myself

Sunshine

Sandy skin

From hugs with strangers

With kind eyes and hearts

Awakening my soul

Opening my eyes

Feeling present once again

— Heather Krystecki

It's Okay to Not Be Okay

Asking for support

Is one of the hardest things

The shame

Guilt

Feeling of inferiority

Why don't I have my life together?

— Heather Krystecki

Sunrise

Sunrise

Beginning of a day

Dark receding to light

Forgetting the past and

Moving forward into the future

Endless possibilities

Our choices

Dictate our futures

How are you going to choose to live each new day?

— Heather Krystecki
