Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

General Questions

What types of photography do you specialize in?

I specialize in wedding photography, portrait sessions, family photography, senior pictures, landscape/fine art photography, and custom projects. Each session is tailored to capture your unique story and vision.

Where are you located?

I'm based in [Your Location], but I'm available for travel throughout the region and beyond. Travel fees may apply for locations outside my local area.

How far in advance should I book?

For weddings, I recommend booking 6-12 months in advance to secure your date. For portrait sessions, 2-4 weeks notice is typically sufficient, though I can sometimes accommodate last-minute requests.

Booking & Pricing

How do I book a session?

You can book a session by visiting our Booking page or contacting me directly. I'll respond within 24-48 hours to discuss your needs and check availability.

What are your rates?

My rates vary depending on the type of session, duration, and deliverables. Please visit the Booking page for detailed package information, or contact me for a custom quote.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes, a 50% non-refundable deposit is required to secure your booking date. The remaining balance is due on or before the session date.

What payment methods do you accept?

I accept credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, Venmo, and bank transfers. Payment details will be provided upon booking confirmation.

Session Details

How long does a typical session last?

Portrait sessions typically last 1-2 hours. Wedding coverage ranges from 6-10 hours depending on your package. Custom projects are quoted based on specific requirements.

Can I choose the location?

Absolutely! I'm happy to shoot at a location of your choice, whether it's your home, a favorite park, or a meaningful venue. I can also suggest locations based on your vision and the type of session.

What should I wear for my session?

Wear something that makes you feel comfortable and confident. I recommend coordinating colors rather than matching exactly, and avoiding busy patterns. I'm happy to provide styling guidance during our consultation.

What happens if it rains on my session day?

For outdoor sessions, we'll monitor the weather and reschedule if necessary at no additional charge. Light rain can actually create beautiful, moody photos if you're up for it!

Photos & Deliverables

When will I receive my photos?

Portrait sessions are typically delivered within 2-3 weeks. Wedding photos are delivered within 6-8 weeks. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee.

How many photos will I receive?

The number varies by package and session type. Portrait sessions typically include 30-50 edited images, while wedding packages include 400-600+ images. All packages include professionally edited, high-resolution digital files.

How will I receive my photos?

Photos are delivered via a private online gallery where you can view, download, share, and order prints. You'll receive high-resolution files suitable for printing and web use.

Do you provide prints or albums?

Yes! I offer professional printing services and custom albums. These can be added to any package or ordered separately after your session.

Can I share my photos on social media?

Absolutely! You have full rights to share and print your photos for personal use. I only ask that you credit HCJK Collection when sharing online.

Wedding Photography

Do you shoot destination weddings?

Yes! I love traveling for weddings. Travel fees and accommodations are additional and will be discussed during our consultation.

Do you have backup equipment?

Yes, I carry backup cameras, lenses, and lighting equipment to every wedding to ensure nothing interrupts your coverage.

Will you work with our videographer?

Absolutely! I'm experienced in coordinating with other vendors to ensure everyone gets the shots they need without getting in each other's way.

Do you offer engagement sessions?

Yes! Engagement sessions are a great way to get comfortable in front of the camera before your wedding day. Many of my wedding packages include a complimentary engagement session.

Technical Questions

What equipment do you use?

I use professional-grade Canon cameras and lenses, along with professional lighting equipment. All equipment is regularly maintained and backed up.

Do you edit all the photos?

Yes, every photo you receive has been professionally edited for color, exposure, and composition. This includes my signature editing style that enhances the natural beauty of each image.

Do you provide RAW files?

RAW files are not included in standard packages as they require professional editing to look their best. However, they can be purchased separately for an additional fee.

Still Have Questions?

I'm here to help! Feel free to reach out with any questions not covered here.
