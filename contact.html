Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

For weddings, I recommend booking 9-12 months in advance, especially for peak season (May-October). For portrait sessions, 2-4 weeks notice is typically sufficient, though I can sometimes accommodate last-minute requests.

What is your service area?

I'm based in [Your Location] and primarily serve [Your Service Area]. However, I'm available for travel anywhere in the world. Travel fees may apply for destinations outside my local area.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! I understand that photography is an investment. I offer flexible payment plans with a deposit to secure your date and the remaining balance due before or on the day of your session.

When will I receive my photos?

Turnaround time varies by session type. Portrait sessions are typically delivered within 2-3 weeks, while weddings take 6-8 weeks due to the extensive editing process. You'll receive a sneak peek within 48 hours!

What happens if it rains on my session day?

For outdoor sessions, we'll monitor the weather closely and reschedule if necessary at no additional charge. Sometimes light rain can create beautiful, moody images—we can discuss your comfort level and preferences.
