Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer signed copies of your book?

Yes! I offer signed copies through my website. These are limited editions and make wonderful gifts. Check the book page for availability and pricing.

Are you available for speaking engagements?

Absolutely! I'm available for mental health events, poetry readings, workshops, corporate wellness programs, and educational institutions. Please email me with details about your event.

Can I share your poetry on social media?

Yes, please do! I love when people share my poetry. Just please credit me (@hcjk_collection) and tag me so I can see it. For commercial use, please contact me for permission.

Do you offer poetry workshops or classes?

Yes, I offer both in-person and online poetry workshops focused on using writing for healing and self-expression. Check my social media or email me for upcoming workshops and availability.

How can I support your work?

There are many ways to support! Buy the book, follow on social media, share my content, leave reviews, and tell others about my work. Every bit of support helps me continue creating and sharing.
