Booking FAQs

How far in advance should I book?

For weddings, I recommend booking 9-12 months in advance. For portrait sessions, 2-4 weeks notice is ideal, though I can sometimes accommodate last-minute requests.

What is your cancellation policy?

The deposit is non-refundable but can be transferred to a new date within one year. Cancellations made more than 60 days before the event receive a 50% refund of payments beyond the deposit.

Do you travel for sessions?

Yes! I'm available for travel anywhere in the world. Travel fees vary based on location and may include transportation, accommodation, and per diem expenses.

When will I receive my photos?

Portrait sessions are delivered within 2-3 weeks. Weddings take 6-8 weeks due to the extensive editing process. You'll receive a sneak peek within 48 hours!
